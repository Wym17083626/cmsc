Voices of Champions

A Collaboration between the Stanford Program in Writing and Rhetoric and the Department of Athletics

Scholar-athletes, those rare and exceptional individuals who combine a thirst for knowledge with a passion for sport, offer a unique perspective on these dual worlds. The Voices of Champions project showcases Stanford scholar-athlete writers and artists through first-person written narratives, profile pieces, artistic performances and multi-media productions. These works provide an up-close glimpse at the scholar-athlete experience. Read more »
