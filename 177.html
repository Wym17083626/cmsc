The Art of the Book in California: Five Contemporary Presses
Opens June 1
An emerging, dynamic art form -- the "new book," is defined by contemporary art practices, successful experiments with media, and innovative structures in book production.  This exhibition includes some of the most significant works from each of five California presses. View press release

Illustrated Title Pages: 1500-1900
Through October 16, 2011
This exhibition of 80 illustrated title pages traces the development of the title page from the 16th to 19th centuries. Title page illustrations by such artists as Giovanni Battista Piranesi, Canaletto, Francisco Goya, William Morris, Odilon Redon, and Aubrey Beardsley are featured in this display.
