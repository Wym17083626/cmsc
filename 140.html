Ways to Give

We stand at the threshold of a pivotal time in medicine, one that has the potential to transform the way we treat and prevent disease. Stanford is dedicated to committing its considerable capabilities to this promise. But we cannot do it alone. Your support will propel us forward faster as we strive to create a healthier, more hopeful future for people everywhere. We invite you to be our partner in this great endeavor.

For further information on giving opportunities, please contact the following development offices:

  • Stanford School of Medicine
  • Stanford Hospital & Clinics
  • Lucile Packard Children's Hospital
