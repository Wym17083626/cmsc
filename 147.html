The Library provides scientifically based medical information to help people make informed decisions about their health and health care. All services are free. Learn More

Your Health: Epilepsy

More than 200,000 people in the U.S. are diagnosed with epilepsy every year, about 1 out of every 100. Robert Fisher, MD, Director of Stanford's Epilepsy Center, explains the different kinds of seizures that present in cases of epilepsy, as well as factors contributing to the condition and current treatments available.
