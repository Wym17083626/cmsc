Referral Centers

  • Stanford Hospital & Clinics Referral Center
    The Stanford Referral Center is staffed Monday through Friday from 8:00am - 5:00pm (PST) with referral specialists who are available to help patients access physicians and services at Stanford Hospital & Clinics. You may contact the Referral Center by calling (800) 756-9000; or e-mail your inquiries to:  referral@stanfordmed.org.

  • Stanford Cancer Center Referrals
    Cancer patients should schedule an appointment at the Stanford Cancer Center. Please contact the referral nurse at (650) 498-6000; or e-mail your inquiries to:  referral@stanfordmed.org.

  • Packard Children's Hospital Referral Center
    The Packard Children's Hospital Referral Center is staffed Monday through Friday from 8 am to 5 pm (PST) with referral specialists who are available to provide information on how to access physicians and services at Lucile Packard Children's Hospital. You may contact the Packard Referral Center by phone at (888) ASK-LPCH (275-5724) ;or e-mail referral@lpch.org.

Referring Physicians

  • Stanford Hospital & Clinics Referring Physician Resource Center
    Physicians can reach the SHC Referring Physician Resource Center at (866) 742-4811, Monday through Friday from 8:00am - 5:00pm (PST). After hours, please call the hospital page operator at (650) 723-4000 and ask for the physician on call.

  • Stanford Cancer Center Referrals
    If you are a physician and wish to refer one of your patients to the Cancer Center, please contact the Referral Center at (650) 498-6000 to schedule an appointment. Referring physicians may also contact new patient coordinators at the phone numbers listed here.

  • Packard Children's Hospital Referral Center
    The LPCH Physician Referral Center is available 24 hours a day, 7 days a week by calling (800) 995-LPCH (5724). More information about programs, services and information designed especially for physicians can be found here.
