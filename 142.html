Alumni Day is Saturday, April 16, 2011
Translating Discoveries: Brain, Body, and Soul

JOIN YOUR CLASSMATES AND COLLEAGUES!
Come back to the School of Medicine campus and join us for a day of learning, reconnecting, and celebrating. All of the morning activities will take place in the Li Ka Shing Center for Learning and Knowledge (LKSC). All grad years are welcome.

Access the Day At a Glance timeline and Alumni Day Brochure.

New this year are two sessions designed for PhD graduates. The day program will be in the new Li Ka Shing Center for Learning and Knowledge (LKSC), which houses a world-class conference center.

Reunion Dinners: If your class year ends in ’01 or ‘06, check the registration site for your class reunion dinner.
