Charles Prober on Stanford's MD Program

Stanford and you

The School of Medicine aims to identify candidates who are committed to serving the needs of all members of society and whose accomplishments reveal originality and a capacity for independent, critical thinking. Our students are best characterized by a strong desire to develop into exceptional clinicians and innovators in the biomedical sciences and public health.
